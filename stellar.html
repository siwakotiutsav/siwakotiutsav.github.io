Chasing Earth’s Shadow

Yesterday was a magical evening in Lawrence, Kansas, as the Moon slipped into Earth’s shadow. I captured this lunar eclipse and created a composite showing each phase of the event.

Gear: Canon EOS 250D, 55–250mm lens, tripod. I adjusted shutter speed and ISO for each phase and processed in Photoshop.

Highlights: Watching the gradual dimming, the dramatic Blood Moon, and reflecting on our cosmic place.

Clear skies and happy stargazing!
— Utsav Siwakoti
