Astronomy Outreach

I am passionate about bringing the universe closer to the public through interactive talks, stargazing sessions, and community events. Some highlights of my outreach work include:

  • Organized monthly public telescope nights at the University of Kansas Observatory (2023–2024)
  • Presented “The James Webb Space Telescope: A New Era in Astronomy” at Lawrence Public Library (2023)
  • Led stargazing sessions for high school students in Nepal during outreach tours (2018–2019)
  • Volunteer speaker at Astronomy Day events, KU Physics & Astronomy Department
