I am a graduate student in the Department of Physics and Astronomy at University of Kansas. I work in the Nearby Galaxy Lab with Prof. Elisabeth Mills.

I am interested in star formation mechanisms, distance mapping, and galactic evolution. Currently, I am using the JWST MIRI dataset to study the star-forming zones of NGC 253. You can find my full CV here.
