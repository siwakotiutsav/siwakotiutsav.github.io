Course Structure (45 Hours)

Mechanics 16 hours
  • Dynamics of System of Particles
  • Work-energy theorem
  • Conservation laws (momentum, angular momentum)
  • Rotational kinematics & moment of inertia
  • Wave and Oscillation: harmonic, damped/forced, resonance
Optics 10 hours
  • Interference: Young's double-slit, thin film, Newton's rings
  • Diffraction: Fraunhofer, diffraction gratings
Thermodynamics 7 hours
  • Heat Transfer: thermal conductivity, black body radiation
  • Thermodynamic Laws: 1st & 2nd law, engines, entropy
Modern Physics 12 hours
  • Atomic & Molecular Physics: spectra, semiconductors
  • Nuclear Physics: reactions, radiation uses, MRI

Required Textbooks

  • Physics I & II by Resnick & Halliday
  • Physics for Engineers by Serway & Jewett
  • Modern Physics by R. Murugeshan
